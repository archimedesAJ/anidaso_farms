Our Company

Anidaso farms is a newly established poultry farm located in Berekuso (adjacent Berekuso CHPS compound) in the Akwapim south municipality of Eastern Region. The farm adopts intensive method of poultry farming where the animals are confined in a brick structure and the best practices in terms of feeding, medications and operations are adopted. Anidaso farms seeks to provide high quality and healthy chicken and eggs to consumers in Ghana at affordable prices. This venture will help create jobs for the youth as people will be employed to work on the farm and market the farmâ€™s product. The venture will also support the aged, youth and some community projects in its quest of giving back to society. Currently the demand for poultry chicken and its related product is very high and the local production cannot meet such huge demand. Therefore, Anidaso Farms entering this sector will help in meeting the rising demand and create jobs for the youth, as the farm is expected to be producing at least 300 chicken and 480 crate of eggs a year in the next five years of operation. Anidaso farms generates it revenue be sales of both chicken (live or dressed) and eggs to customers in Berekuso, Kitaase, Ayim, Agyementi, Comet, Kwabenya and Pokuase.

"We focus on delivering nutritious and high-quality eggs and chickens to Ghanaians at affordable prices"

At Anidaso farms, we hold on dearly to our mantra; Quality Products for Healthy Living. We seek to ensure that our cherish clients are served the best and high-quality poultry products. We seek to beat the odds to become the leading producer and supplier of quality eggs and chicken in Ghana.
